What is KCC?

The Kerala Campus Conference is a distinguished event initiated by the Jesus Youth Kerala Campus Ministry. It aims to unite active campus ministers from across Kerala.

Provides leaders with enhanced clarity regarding the ministry's current activities and focuses. Effectively communicates the ministry's vision to grass root levels.

Hundreds of young leaders who share their experiences in building the kingdom of God on campuses.

Serves as a vital platform for igniting the spirit of evangelization in the leadership for the years to come.

Steps to Register

  1. Request an Invitation

    Contact your college coordinator to get your invitation.

  2. Download the KCC App

    Once invited, download and install the KCC app to complete your registration.

  3. Join the KCC Community

    Engage with the community through the app, participate in prayers, and stay updated.

  4. Pay the Registration Fee

    You'll be notified when the portal opens. Pay the fee to secure your spot.

  5. Prepare for KCC24

    You're all set! Get ready for an amazing experience.
